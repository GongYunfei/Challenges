Math for left columns physical width.

​
225px (Content Width)
+ 40px (left + right padding)
+ 20px (left + right border)
+ 40px (20px left + 20px right margin)
= 325px (physical width)

Math for right columns physical width.

​
187px (Content Width)
+ 40px (left + right padding)
+ 20px (left + right border)
+ 40px (20px left + 20px right margin)
= 287px (physical width)

Define Physical Width Versus Content Width:


Content Width:

It is the width of the content of a block.




Physical Width:

It is the sum of left and right margin width, two times of border width, left and right padding width, content width of a block.
